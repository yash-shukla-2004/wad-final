PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

Engineering Mathematics I
Calculus, matrices, and vector algebra fundamentals.
Basic Electrical Engineering
Basics of circuits, networks, and electrical machines.
Engineering Physics
Optics, waves, and quantum principles for engineers.

Second Year (SE)

Data Structures
Concepts of arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, and trees.
Computer Organization
Microprocessors, instruction sets, and memory hierarchy.
Discrete Mathematics
Set theory, logic, relations, and combinatorics.

Third Year (TE)

Operating Systems
Concepts of process scheduling, memory, and file systems.
Database Management Systems
Relational models, SQL, normalization, and transactions.
Computer Networks
OSI model, protocols, routing, and network devices.

Final Year (BE)

Artificial Intelligence
Introduction to AI, machine learning, and neural networks.
Cloud Computing
Virtualization, cloud models, and infrastructure as a service.
Project Phase I & II
Development of a full-scale academic project over two semesters.
